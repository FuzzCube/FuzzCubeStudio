THE FINAL STEP OF GREAT IMPORTANCE Mastering is the final step of audio post-production. The purpose of mastering is to balance sonic elements of a stereo mix and optimize playback across all systems and media formats. Traditionally, mastering is done using tools like equalization, compression, limiting and stereo enhancement. DO NO HARM My main principle in working on the master is DO NO HARM. SHAPING THE DETAILS OF YOUR TRACK Stem Mastering is a process that offers greater control over the sonic shape and the balance of your music. It allows an audio engineer to not only to shape the overall tonal balance of a track but also to go deeper into each part and to achieve an even better result. Each stem is treated with the combination of different processes and effects to help the track to achieve its absolute potential.
